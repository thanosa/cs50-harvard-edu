This web-site teaches you the LBL (Layer-by-Layer) method to solve Rubic's cube.

This is an easy method for Rookie cubers.

Layer-By-Layer, or just LBL is a group of methods that solves the cube in layers.
In the LBL method, the solver finishes 1 layer at a time:

  • The 1st layer
    • The cross
    • The corners
  • The 2nd layer
    • The edges
  • The 3rd last layer
    • The cross
    • The edges
    • The corners - positions
    • The corners - orientation
